Wayne Gretzky

Biography

Wayne Gretzky Wayne Gretzky is a famous hockey player born in Brantford, Ontario on January 26th, 1961.  He is known to be the greatest hockey player that ever played the game, thus his nickname "The Great One".  He played 20 seasons in the NHL for four teams, including the Edmonton Oilers, Los Angeles Kings, St. Louis Blues and New York Rangers from 1979 to 1999.  He still holds many statistical records in the NHL including most goals in a regular season being 92 goals scored.  He retired in 1999 with the New York Rangers.  His #99 was retired by the National Hockey League meaning that no other player may ever wear that number in the NHL again.
